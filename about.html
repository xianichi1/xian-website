Hey, I'm Xian

Thanks for checking out my site! I made this because I’ve always been curious about why cats do the things they do. You know—those little quirks, weird habits, and funny moods they get into? I wanted a space to figure that stuff out and share what I learn.

Most of this is based on my own experiences and research, and honestly just spending a lot of time hanging out with cats and paying attention. I’m not a vet or anything—just someone who really loves learning how to connect with them better.

I’m currently a second-year Graphic Design student. I’m 20 years old while building this, and I also love basketball, fashion, and (obviously) cats. This site is kind of my creative corner for blending all that together.

Fun Facts About Me

  • My favorite color is purple
