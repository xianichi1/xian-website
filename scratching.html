How to Stop Scratching & Biting

Scratching and biting are normal cat behaviors, but they can be frustrating—especially when directed at you or your furniture. Here’s how to manage them with patience and care:

  • Provide Scratching Alternatives: Give your cat scratching posts or pads and place them where they already like to scratch.
  • Use Deterrents: Try double-sided tape, citrus sprays, or furniture covers to discourage scratching specific areas.
  • Trim Their Nails: Regular nail trimming reduces damage from scratching. Just be gentle and careful!
  • Play Gently: If your cat bites during play, pause immediately. This helps them learn boundaries.
  • Watch for Triggers: Overstimulation, fear, or stress can lead to biting. Pay attention to body language.

Never punish your cat. Instead, redirect their energy and reinforce good behavior. Over time, you’ll see real improvements!
